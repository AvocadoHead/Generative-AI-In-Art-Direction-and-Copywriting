The shift: from “copy here, visual there” to one unified canvas.

Most of the industry still works in a “divide and fragment” mindset. Copy in one tab, images in another, and the original brief gets lost somewhere in the middle. The result: friction. Too many technical handoffs, not enough continuous creation.

This workshop moves you into a unified workflow. Forget ping‑ponging between tools. We work on a single generative canvas, where written strategy and visual output evolve side by side. It does not matter if you use Krea, ComfyUI, or Runway – the real shift is in your thinking, not in the tool.

The methodology: from brief to video in 4 movements.

The methodology: from brief to video in 4 movements.

A structured workflow that turns creative chaos into a precise pipeline.

Deconstruction // Understanding the DNA

The brief does not go into the machine as‑is. First, it is taken apart into its core components.

Learn: How to surface hidden intentions and brand values using LLMs, and how to spot the gap between “what the client asked for” and “what the brand actually needs”.

Decoding // Building the language

Turning insights into a creative operating system. This is where words become visuals.

Learn: Building an AI‑driven style‑sheet, translating narrative into composition, and crafting prompts that do more than describe an image – they tell a story.

Mutation // The big bang

No random spraying of ideas – instead, an evolution of concepts.

Learn: Text → Image → Text cycles, smart variations, and using mood exploration to reach results that are surprising yet precise.

Refinement // The final reduction

The distillation phase. Sketches become assets, ideas become production‑ready.

Learn: Building visual consistency across series, iterative refinement, and preparing for full production: from storyboard to video shots.
